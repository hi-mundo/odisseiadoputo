The Odyssey of the Puto is a nerd blog: tech, development, and whatever comes to mind — written by people on the front line.

What does “puto” mean?

Formal version: Platform Utilitarian Theoretical and Original. The version for anyone who’s been there: imagine being in a war room for 12 hours fixing a problem caused by a dev who got too excited about Uncle Bob’s bible.

What you’ll find here

On the home page there’s a filter by post type. We group posts like this:

  • Code — dev, DevOps, SRE, software architecture, tutorials and technical opinion.
  • News — commentary on tech news.
  • Pop — games, movies, series and pop culture in general.

In short: a bit of everything from the nerd menu. In posts, use tags: [codigo], tags: [noticias] or tags: [pop] in the front matter (or more than one) for the home filter to work.
